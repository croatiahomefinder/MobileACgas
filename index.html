Symptoms & what we do

Air con not cold? Here's the fix

Most systems lose a little gas every year. When it drops far enough, cooling fades — and a proper regas brings it back.

Signs you need a regas

  • ✓Air blows warm or only slightly cool
  • ✓Slow to get cold, or warms up in traffic
  • ✓Weak airflow even on full fan
  • ✓Musty or damp smell when you switch it on
  • ✓Compressor cuts in and out, or won't engage
  • ✓Windows take longer to demist

What's included in a regas

  • ✓Recover & weigh any existing refrigerant
  • ✓Vacuum & leak hold to check the system is sealed
  • ✓Recharge to factory weight with correct PAG oil
  • ✓UV leak dye added so future leaks are easy to trace
  • ✓Performance test on vent temps & pressures
  • ✓Written report handed over at the end

FAQ

Air con regas questions, answered

How much does a mobile air con regas cost?
R134a regas starts from £69.99 and R1234yf from £99. The final figure depends on your vehicle and refrigerant type, and we confirm it before booking — no surprises.
Do you really come to me?
Yes — we're fully mobile and do the whole job at your home, work or yard, on your own drive, anywhere across Birmingham and the West Midlands.
What vehicles can you regas?
Cars, vans, trucks, HGVs, tractors, motorhomes, buses and plant or agricultural machinery — the same refrigerants are used across all of them.
How long does it take?
Around 45–60 minutes from arrival to handover, including the vacuum leak-test and a performance check.
What if my system has a leak?
Every recharge includes UV dye, and the vacuum hold-test flags major leaks before we gas it. If we find a fault it goes in your report and we explain the options rather than refilling a leaking system.
